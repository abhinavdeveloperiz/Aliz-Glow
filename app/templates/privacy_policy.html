Privacy Policy

We use your data to process orders, improve our website, send promotional updates, and enhance customer service. Your personal data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

Our website uses cookies to personalize your browsing experience and analyze site performance. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies at any time.

4. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your payment details are processed securely through trusted third-party payment gateways.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To make a request, please contact us at contact@alizglow.com.

Aliz Glow reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
